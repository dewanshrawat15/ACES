What Is ACES..??


Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is the student body of Computer Engineering Department, BV(DU)COE Pune which provides a platform for the holistic development of a Computer Engineer. Here we not only learn the technicalities of a particular thing but also embrace its social and cultural aspects for the greater good of the society. Apart from working in the department on various things like organising technical events and workshops we also work towards a better society by organising social events and workshops.
